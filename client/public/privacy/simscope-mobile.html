Privacy Policy – SimScope Mobile

Last updated: 01 Nov 2025 • Owner: Sim Scope Inc. • Contact: simscopeinc@gmail.com

Overview

SimScope Mobile (“the App”) is provided by Sim Scope Inc. (“we”, “us”). This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Account & contact data you provide (name, email) to sign in or receive invoices/notifications.
  • App content you choose to upload (job notes, photos). Photos are used only inside your account.
  • Device permissions:
    • Camera / Photos – attach job photos.
    • Microphone – voice notes/calls (if you use these features).
    • Push notifications – job updates and reminders.
    • We do not collect precise location unless a feature explicitly asks and you consent.
  • Diagnostics: basic analytics and crash logs to improve stability.

How We Use Data

  • Provide core features (jobs, invoices, media attachments, notifications).
  • Customer support and troubleshooting.
  • Security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

We do not sell your personal data. Sharing is limited to service providers needed to run the App (e.g., hosting and push services) under appropriate agreements.

Data Retention

We keep data for as long as your account is active or as needed for business and legal purposes. You may request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your data.
  • Withdraw permissions in device settings (camera, photos, microphone, notifications).
  • Contact us to export or erase your account data.

Children

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
